  • 04 The way you say something — precisely. Not approximately. You find the exact word. Most people don't bother. You always do.
  • 05 Your cooking has a standard. It's not written down anywhere — it's just in your head. And if something doesn't meet it, you know immediately. That's not common.
  • 06 The real smile. Not the one for photos. The one that happens half a second before — when something actually lands. That's the one I keep watching for.
  • 07 You put in the work. Quietly, fully, without announcing it. And then the thing is just… done. Better than expected. Every time.
